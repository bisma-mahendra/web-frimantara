How Do We Export?

Shipping and Payment

Shipping

EXWORK

The costs borne by the buyer include delivery costs from our warehouse in Bogor Regency to the buyer’s place.

FOB

The costs borne by the buyer include shipping costs when the goods are transported to the ship at the port of Tanjung Priok to the destination country.

Payment

Advance Payment

This advance payment can be made via Telegraphic Transfer (T/T)

Irrevocable Confirmed L/C

Payment for this L/C is fully guaranteed by both the exporter’s bank and the importer’s bank
